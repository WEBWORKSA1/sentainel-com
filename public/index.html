The Moat

The NIST AI RMF safe-harbor matrix.

Every Sentainel obligation maps to NIST AI RMF controls. When your auditor asks "how do we know you complied?" — you hand them the matrix. 341 obligation×control mappings across 9 regulations.

Atomic granularity, not framework hand-waving.

Competitors stop at "GDPR Article 22." Sentainel maps every operative clause — quoted, dated, confidence-scored. That's the difference between a slide and a defense.

Multi-jurisdiction by design.

One product, four jurisdictions, nine regulations, 59 obligations. TXAIMS does Texas only. CO-AIMS does Colorado only. Augure does Quebec only. Sentainel does all of them.

NIST safe-harbor evidence.

Both Texas TRAIGA and Ontario's IPC AI Scribe Guidance recognize NIST AI RMF alignment as a defense. The Sentainel matrix is your pre-built evidence package.
