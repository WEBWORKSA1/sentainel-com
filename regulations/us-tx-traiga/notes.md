## Sales objection handling

**Objection: "TRAIGA isn't really enforced yet."**
Response: Complaint portal due September 1, 2026. § 552.103
documentation requirements exist now regardless of complaint
volume. Sectoral agencies (TMB etc.) can enforce § 552.106 sanctions
independent of AG action.

**Objection: "We don't have a Texas office."**
Response: § 551.002 has three independent triggers. If you produce
a product or service used by Texas residents, you're subject to
TRAIGA regardless of physical Texas presence — same
extraterritorial reach as Quebec Law 25.

**Objection: "We already do NIST AI RMF."**
Response: Self-implementation requires documented evidence,
internal review processes, ongoing risk assessments, and
audit-defensible records. Sentainel automates the documentation
layer and maintains the evidence repository at audit-ready quality.
