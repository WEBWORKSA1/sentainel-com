# Council Astroturfing Response Procedure — White Paper Reference

The procedure originally developed in this Thorold demonstration audit bundle (see [`03-aia-template.md`](./03-aia-template.md) Part B) has been extracted, expanded, and published as a standalone white paper by the Sentainel Compliance Research Group.

**Canonical source for the procedure as a publishable artifact:**
[`whitepapers/council-astroturfing-response-procedure-v1.md`](../../../whitepapers/council-astroturfing-response-procedure-v1.md)

## What's in the white paper that isn't in the AIA template

The AIA template at `03-aia-template.md` Part B was written for Thorold-specific adoption. The white paper extracts the procedure as a general framework for any Ontario municipality, and adds:

- A full Abstract and Contents structured for an institutional readership (AMO, AMCTO, Municipal World, IPC counsel, OHRC observers, municipal lawyers)
- A formal regulatory framing across all four layers of the Ontario AI compliance stack (§3)
- A threat-model analysis covering campaign architecture, detection difficulty, and the cost-asymmetry problem (§4)
- Detailed implementation guidance with resource estimates and adoption pathway (§6)
- An Anticipated Objections and Responses section addressing seven likely critiques in plain language (§7)
- A Limitations and Scope section setting honest boundaries on what the procedure does and does not do (§8)
- A Comparative Analysis covering U.S., E.U., and Quebec adjacent jurisdictions (§9)
- A Model Council Resolution (Annex A) ready for adaptation by adopting municipalities
- An Intake Log Field Schema (Annex B)
- A Pattern Review Template (Annex C)
- A Glossary (Annex D)

## Which document to use when

- **For Thorold-specific adoption** (responding to the actual 2024–2025 incident): use the AIA template at `03-aia-template.md` Part B. It is calibrated to Thorold's specific context.
- **For any other Ontario municipality considering adoption**: use the white paper. It is the general framework.
- **For AMO conference submissions, Municipal World articles, IPC commentary, or other publication contexts**: cite the white paper.
- **For internal Sentainel use or municipal demo bundles**: cross-reference both.

The two documents will be kept consistent. The white paper is the canonical procedure description going forward; the Thorold AIA template will be revised in future demo bundle updates to point at the white paper as the source of record for the procedure itself.
